How I Work

Whatever I’m building, I follow the same simple approach.

1

Understand the system

I start by clarifying the real problem, constraints, equipment, users, and expected outcome.

2

Organize the details

I turn messy information, notes, manuals, or requirements into clear structures.

3

Prototype and test

I use CAD, calculations, lab tools, simulations, and hands-on testing to validate ideas.

4

Improve with evidence

I refine based on feedback, safety factors, fit-up issues, data, and real operating conditions.
